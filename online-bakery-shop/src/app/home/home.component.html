Trove is a gourmet dessert company that is the manifestation of Karli and Julia's lifetime passion of all things dessert.

Years of supplying increasingly refined cakes, pies, cookies, and squares to family events has transformed from a passion into a gourmet dessert company.

Our mission with Trove Desserts is to create exceptional dessert experiences that are worth sharing. We make a holiday, a party, or simply a Monday feel special.
